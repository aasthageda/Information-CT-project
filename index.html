What is the Darknet?

The Darknet refers to a part of the internet not indexed by traditional search engines and often requires specialized software like Tor.

Darknet vs Deep Web vs Surface Web

  • Surface Web: Accessible through search engines like Google.
  • Deep Web: Content not indexed, like private databases and email accounts.
  • Darknet: Requires special tools and is often associated with privacy-focused activities.

Risks and Ethical Concerns

While the Darknet is used for privacy, it is also associated with illegal activities. Users should be aware of the legal and ethical considerations.
